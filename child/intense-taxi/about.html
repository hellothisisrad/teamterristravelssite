History

1994, John Smith

City-Taxi was founded in 1994 by John Smith, a former taxi driver and current entrepreneur. After working for more than 10 years in transportation industry, he decided to establish his own company, which could reflect his interests in the industry of civil transportation.

As a company that operates throughout the USA with a vast and developed partner network, City-Taxi provides taxicab services in big cities, at many central airports, and other locations. Our service range is constantly developing, and that’s one of the reasons why people love us.

Taxis and cabs have always been my passion since I entered this industry. I’m glad that my dream has not only become a reality, but also helps people all over the US to reach the goals they set using City-Taxi. I promise that in the future our services will only be better!

FAQ

How do I pre-book a cab?

You can pre-book a cab through our website or by calling our office at toll free number which you can find on our website. Or simply download our App on your Android phone or iPhone - it is the easiest way to book a cab.

How can I order a taxi?

Ordering our taxi is easy! Since you are already here you can book your trip now using our website. Ordering a taxi online saves you time, because the details of your trip are automatically entered. Or you can book a taxi on your smartphone or mobile device, using City-Taxi’s booking app.

How can I pay for my transfer in the taxi?

We accept cash and major credit cards - Visa, MasterCard, and American Express.

How do I recognize your taxis?

All our taxis are dark yellow and they have our logo clearly displayed both on the bonnet and on the sides. On the lantern you should look for a yellow circle in the middle off the word TAXI.
